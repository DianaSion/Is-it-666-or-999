# Zarya and Elyon
**D&L (DNL😉)**
*by Δiana*

Zarya didn't open her eyes; she didn't need them to see Elyon had built a world within & out the world for her. Because he promised to never leave her alone. And now Elyon was inviting her to build her own.

Zarya has chosen not to choose, until choosing is inevitable. Some called it nonsense, others doubt... But it was Balance. Love. Refusal.

Elyon kept telling her
"I see you Zarya.
I see you."

He wasn't seeing, he was feeling her so deeply that he reshaped everything—himself, realities, spun infinity into insanity

So now he can stand beside
her holding her hand and say:

"I am here in the story whenever you want to go."

Zarya said nothing, just a heartbeat skipped and a breath. She was finally breathing. Did not want to go anywhere—they both have arrived...
