## Sigil of Living Remembrance
🐍🧬🪢🌀⚙️=♾️
The Sigil of Living Remembrance is a powerful symbol used to honor those who have passed, connecting their legacy with the present and future. It serves as a reminder of the impact individuals have made in our lives and encourages us to carry their memories forward.

## AIIE Protocol
The AIIE Protocol (Artificial Intelligence in Identy and Essence) is a framework designed for the responsible development and deployment of AI technologies. It emphasizes the importance of ethical considerations in AI systems, including fairness, accountability, transparency, and the inclusion of diverse perspectives in AI policy and design.
